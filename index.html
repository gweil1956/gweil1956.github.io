Microring Resonator

Resonant Filter

Add-drop microring resonator simulated on a silicon-on-insulator platform using Tidy3D FDTD. A silicon ring (radius 4 µm, width 400 nm) is evanescently coupled to two parallel bus waveguides with a 150 nm gap. A broadband Gaussian mode source launched from the input port drives resonances across the 1.5–1.6 µm telecom band. Flux monitors at the through and drop ports capture the transmission spectrum, revealing periodic resonance dips with ~25–27 dB extinction and a free spectral range of approximately 20 nm.

Edge Coupler

I/O Interface

Linear-taper waveguide coupler simulated on a silicon-on-insulator platform using Tidy3D FDTD. The waveguide narrows from 500 nm at the output to 350 nm at the input facet over a 5 µm taper, improving mode overlap with the input field. A broadband Gaussian pulse spanning 760–840 nm (center 800 nm) drives a ModeSource, and power transmission into each of 5 output modes is tracked via a ModeMonitor at the output. The simulation domain is terminated with PML on all sides with a 1 ps run time and 10⁻⁵ shutoff criterion.
