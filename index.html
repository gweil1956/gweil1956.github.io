2.4 GHz Dual-Element Phased Array RF Front-End

In Progress

A 2-element 2.4 GHz phased array demo board with integrated patch antennas, controlled-impedance microstrip routing, and digital phase control via the PE43204. Validated through VNA S-parameter measurement and near-field beam pattern characterization across a ±45° steering range.

Design Rationale

Substrate Selection. FR4 was selected because at 2.4 GHz and short trace lengths the dielectric loss is acceptable, while keeping cost low and fabrication simple.
Array Size. Two elements were chosen to demonstrate phase-controlled beam steering while keeping layout symmetry, coupling, and phase matching manageable.
Phase Control. Digital phase shifting was used to enable programmable steering with sufficient resolution to meet the angular range and phase error targets.
Integrated Antennas. Patch antennas were integrated on the PCB to control spacing, impedance transitions, and electrical length without added connector discontinuities.
